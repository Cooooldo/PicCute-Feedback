隐私政策

最后更新：2026-04-23。本政策适用于 PicCute（macOS）。如本政策与应用内说明不一致，以最新发布版本和本页面更新内容为准。

1. 我们收集哪些信息

  • PicCute 不要求注册账号，不建立用户档案，不出售个人信息。
  • 你用于压缩的图片文件默认在本地设备处理，不上传到我们的服务器。
  • 当你主动通过 GitHub Issues 或邮件联系我们时，我们会收到你提交的联系方式与问题内容。

2. 应用权限与本地访问

  • 应用仅访问你明确选择或拖入的文件与文件夹，用于读取、压缩和输出结果。
  • 若为 Mac App Store 版本，系统可能提示文件夹访问授权；你可在系统设置中随时管理权限。

3. 数据用途

  • 完成你发起的图片压缩、格式转换、输出命名与结果展示。
  • 在你主动反馈问题时，用于定位故障、沟通修复进度与回复支持请求。

4. 第三方服务

  • 应用本身不要求登录第三方账号。
  • 如果你点击反馈链接跳转 GitHub 或发送邮件，则相关平台会按其各自隐私政策处理数据。

5. 数据保留与安全

  • 压缩输入与输出文件由你在本地设备自行保留和管理。
  • 通过邮件或 GitHub 提交的支持信息，会在问题处理和必要留档期间保留。

6. 儿童隐私

本应用不面向 13 岁以下儿童，也不会主动收集儿童个人信息。

7. 政策更新

我们可能随产品变化更新本政策，更新后将发布在此页面并更新“最后更新”日期。

8. 联系我们

如有隐私相关问题，请联系：aimeng661@gmail.com

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-23. This policy applies to PicCute (macOS). If there is any mismatch between this page and older in-app text, the latest published version and this page apply.

1. What we collect

  • PicCute does not require account registration, does not build user profiles, and does not sell personal information.
  • Images you compress are processed locally on your device by default and are not uploaded to our servers.
  • If you contact us via GitHub Issues or email, we receive the contact details and content you choose to provide.

2. Permissions and local access

  • The app only accesses files and folders you explicitly select or drag into the app to read, compress, and write outputs.
  • For the Mac App Store build, macOS may ask for folder access permission; you can manage permissions in system settings at any time.

3. How data is used

  • To perform compression, format conversion, output naming, and result display requested by you.
  • To investigate issues and respond to support requests when you proactively submit feedback.

4. Third-party services

  • The app itself does not require third-party account sign-in.
  • If you follow links to GitHub or send email, those platforms process data under their own privacy policies.

5. Retention and security

  • Your input and output image files remain under your local control.
  • Support messages sent via email or GitHub may be retained for troubleshooting and minimal record-keeping.

6. Children's privacy

This app is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Policy updates

We may update this policy as the product evolves. Any update will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
